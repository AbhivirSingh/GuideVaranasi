Dhamek Stupa Sarnath

Sarnath

Sarnath is the place where Buddha preached his first sermon after enlightenment in Bodh Gaya. It is a great pilgrimage centre for Buddhist. First of all your tourist guide will take you to Srilankan Temple where you will find a golden statue of Buddha and nice wall paintings representing the life of Buddha. After that, you will visit Archeological Site where you will see Dhamekh Stupa which was made by Ashoka in the 3rd century BC. It represents the place where Buddha preached his first century. Then you will visit the Archeological Museum of Sarnath where you will see the national emblem(four lions) of India and many statues of Buddha and Hindu Gods. Finally, you will see big Buddha(80 feet)

Aarti Varanasi

Ganga Aarti

Aarti, the prayer ceremony of the Ganges river which takes place every day on the bank of the Ganges river. It is performed on a stage by a group of young Hindu priests. Chanting of hymns and blowing of the conch shell marks the start of the Aarti after that priests do this ceremony with incenses, flowers, and fire. More or less 12000 pilgrims gather there to see the Aarti. The Aarti starts after the sunset at around 6:30 pm in the winter season (7:00 pm in the summer season) and is a forty-five-minute long soulful ritual.
